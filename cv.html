Educational Outreach, Teaching & Curriculum Development

  • Trained and supervised 150+ graduate and undergraduate student interns and employees in data analysis, marketing communications, and experiential financial education (2011–Present).
  • Developed and delivered financial education for undergraduate and graduate populations in-person and virtually across three universities (2011–Present).

Research & Program Evaluation

  • Led data analysis projects implementing data mining and machine learning techniques (2012–Present), including analysis of participation patterns in the Financial Literacy Badges Program (Spring 2020) and evaluation of educational interventions for UIC students (Fall 2019).
  • Created an AI-enabled chatbot to conduct qualitative interviews using Python, building off research by Geiecke & Jaravel (2024), as part of mixed methods dissertation study (2025).
  • Evaluated multi-year student loan education effectiveness with embedded vs. disconnected visualizations using administrative data and survey responses across several intervention periods (2018–2023).
  • Conducted needs assessment using enrollment, financial, and demographics data for past due students (2016–2017).
  • Conducted analyses of UISAVES outreach, including savings competition participation (2012–2021).
  • Analyzed 5-year outcomes of financial literacy requirement for past due students, finding no consistent improvement in payment behavior (2010–2015).
