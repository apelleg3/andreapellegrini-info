Dissertation Defense

Defending April 3, 2026 at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

Expected May 2026 · Defending April 3

Goal Framing & Visualizations' Influence on Self-Regulated Learning of Compound Interest: An Explanatory Mixed Methods Study

My research explores how visual media (data visualizations, visual metaphors, and UI design elements) affecting self-regulated learning in adult financial education.

For my dissertation, I used an explanatory mixed-methods design to investigate data visualizations in learning compound interest, with an LLM conducting follow-up participant interviews.
