Research

PhD Work & Dissertation

My research connects cognitive science, visual media, and adult financial education, specifically how people learn and self-regulate when visuals are part of the experience.

Dissertation · Expected May 2026

Goal Framing & Visualizations' Influence on Self-Regulated Learning of Compound Interest: An Explanatory Mixed Methods Study

My research examines how visual media affects self-regulated learning in adult financial education, using an explanatory mixed-methods design with an LLM to conduct participant interviews.
